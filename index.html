Purpose of this hub

This hub is designed to make Ahmed Mansour's published indoor positioning research easier to discover, understand, cite, and reuse by researchers, practitioners, and AI-assisted literature search systems.

  • Dedicated publication pages with DOI, BibTeX, keywords, and citation-use notes.
  • Research themes linking crowdsensing, radio maps, Wi-Fi reliability, IOD, PDR, RTLS, and spatial intelligence.
  • Machine-readable metadata for search engines, academic retrieval systems, and AI search tools.
  • Links to accepted manuscripts, datasets, code, supplementary tables, and benchmarks when available.
