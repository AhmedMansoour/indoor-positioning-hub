About Ahmed Mansour

Ahmed Mansour is a Post-Doctoral Fellow at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Department of Land Surveying and Geo-Informatics, and an Assistant Professor at Cairo University, Egypt. His research interests include indoor positioning, smartphone positioning, pedestrian positioning, Wi-Fi positioning, crowdsourcing, seamless navigation, indoor-outdoor detection, multi-sensor fusion, and cooperative positioning.
